Gundon se nipatne ke liye fauj ki nahi, himmat ki zaroorat padti hai
Zindagi mein insaan do hi baar gehri neend sota hai  maut ki gaud mein yah maa ke aanchal mein
Tann pe jitne kapde hai  ussi ko main apna kafan maanke chalta hoon
Jisne bhi mooh dikhane ki jurrat ki  uski mulaqat maut se hogi
Maine usse kitna yaad kiya  yeh toh meri yaadash ko bhi yaad nahi
Paraye ko gale lagana aasan hota hai  par apnana bahut mushkil
