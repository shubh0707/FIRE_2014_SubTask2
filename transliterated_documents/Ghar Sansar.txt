Is sansar mein bheek le lo magar udhaar kabhi mat lo  kyun ki bheek leke aadmi ek baar zaleel hota hai  magar udhaar maangke subah shyam zaleel hota hai
Aksar desi aankhon par videshi chasma lagane se  dimaag mein charbi chad jaati hai
Joh bhi kaam mile usse kar lena chahiye  kaam koi chota nahi hota  chota hota hai aadmi, uska dil, uski kartoot
Badi tedhi hai yeh duniya  yahan haqdaar ko bhi apna haq bheek ki tarah maangna padta hai  anpad naukri maangne jaye toh usse degree lane ke liye kehte hai  degree lekar jaye toh waseela lane ke liye kehte hai  aur waseelay ke liye kisi ke paas jaye toh rishvat maangte hai
Haath woh kalam hai joh aakash ke kagaz pe likhi hui taqdeer ko badal sakta hai
Badhon ke mooh se nikli hui gaaliyan  choton ko dua bankar lagti hai  joh badhon ki daant kha leta hai  woh zindagi ki thokarein nahi khata
Daulat waalon ki duniya mein baimaani ka daakhla band hai  baimaani agar aati hai toh noton ka naqab audkar imaandari ban jaati hai
Ghatiya sawaal poochna bahut aasaan hai  lekin unka jawaab dena bahut mushkil  joh tum jaisa ghatiya aadmi bhi nahi de sakta
Main woh khota sikka hoon  joh dikhne mein toh ek anni hai lekin chalne mein rupaiya hai
Bhookha rehkar aadmi ek baar marta hai  lekin udhaar ki roti khakar baar baar marta hai  hazaar baar marta hai
Jis ghar sansar ki gaadi udhaar ke paiyon pe chalti hai  woh subah shyam puncture hoti chali jaati hai
Jab tab ungli tedhi na karo, dibbe mein se ghee nahi nikalta  aur jab tak tedha rasta na pakdo, bhigda hua sahi raste par nahi aata
