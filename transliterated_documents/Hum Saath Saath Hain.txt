Hum chahe bandhan mein bandhe na bandhe  jazbaat ka rishta toh dil se hai
Ladki apne dil ki baat aankhon se keh deti hai
Jisse hum jhagadte hai  pyar bhi toh sabse zyada ussi ko karte hai
Tumne aurat ko samjha nahi bhaiya  woh andhero se nahi darti  jab woh faisla kar leti hai toh mann mein vishvas ka diya jalakar  usse apni maang ka sindoor bana leti hai
Aadmi ko subah jaldi uthna chahiye  varna saare kaam adhoore reh jate hai
Bachpan ki mohabbat ko dil se na juda karna  jab yaad meri aaye milne ki dua karna
Jitna sukh saath rehne mein hai  utna alag rehne mein nahi
Satta milne par pyare se pyara insaan bhi apne rang badal leta hai
Insaan ki pehchaan tann se nahi  mann se ki jati hai
The family that prays together, eats together  stays together
Agar kisika dukh baatna hi chahte ho  toh kaam kuch aisa karo  ke doosro ki zindagi mein khushi aaye
Bhabhijaan, aap hum mein apnaye na apnaye  hum aapko mooh dikhayi mein mile hai
Joh kaam aaj poore nahi hue woh kal poore honge  har subah apne saath asha ki ek nayi kiran lekar aati hai
