Mohabbat ussi ka naam hai joh insaan ko qurbaaniya sikhati hai
Zehereli nagin sabke liye zeher rakhti hai  magar woh bhi apne naag ke liye pyar rakhti hai
Samajhdar log kabhi dil se mohabbat nahi karte  woh hamesha dimag se mohabbat karte hai
Koi haarkar jeet leta hai aur koi jeetkar haar jaata hai
