Is desh ke gaddaron ko anjaam tak pahunchana  yahi meri zindagi ka maqsad hai
Joh markar zinda hote hai  unki zindagi haramzadi ho jaati hai  joh na aasani se marti hai aur na hi kisiko aasan maut maarti hai
Jab ek fauji ki khopdi garam ho jaati hai toh woh apni hadh hi kya  dushman ki sarhad bhi paar kar jaata hai
Chori se liya gaya chumma  zindagi ki sabse haseen cheez hoti hai
Tum jaise gaddaron ka anth ek alag andaz se hoga
