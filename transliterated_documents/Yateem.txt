Ghar na toh char deewaron ka naam hai aur na un char deewaron ke andhar rehne waalon ka  ghar toh us pyar ka naam hai joh hawaon mein ghula hua hai
Tere paap toh itne ghinone hai  ki daya ka sagar bhagwan bhi tujhe maaf karne se pehle do baar sochega
Hatyare se bada paapi woh hai  joh kisi masoom ko hatyara banata hai
Farishtay ke ghar mein shaitaan pehda hota hai  aur shaitaan ke ghar mein farishta
