Pyar puja ke barabar hota hai
Bhagwan ke prasad ko na karna paap hai
Zindagi complications ka combination hai
Gareeb ko sirf roti ka sapna dekhna chahiye  pyar ka nahi
Kutte ko darwaaze ke bahar hi baithna chahiye  aur aadmi ko ghar ke andar hi
Agar pati achcha na ho, toh aulad ke bharose jee sakte hai  aur agar aulad galat raaste par chalne lage, toh pati ke bharose jee sakte hai
Woh log bhi is duniya mein nahi rahe  joh dharm ki deewar khadi karke pyar ke aade aaye
Gussa karne wali maa se gumrah karne wala baap kabhi bada nahi hota
