Yam hai hum  hum hai Yam!
Cheena jhapti, loot-mar, atyachar, brashtachar, balatkar  aaisa koi paap nahi joh is manav ne nah kiya ho
Bhagwan sansar mein har jagah har ghadi nahi reh sakta  is liye usne apne badle ghar ghar mein maa ko pehda kiya hai
Choro se maal bachane ke liye dukaan ko taala lagaya ja sakta hai  lekin dokhebaazo se bachna bahut hi mushkil hai
Yeh sharab ka pehla usool hai  jiski piyo sharab, uska hi ghar karo kharab
Aadhi raat ko bulana, subah tak zaroor aaonga yeh meri arz hai  shehar mein shanti aur nagriko ki seva karna mera farz hai
Humri ek choti si behna, chhod rahi hai aaj humra angna  uski shaadi hai humpe udhaar, karoonga main uski shaadi bar-bar, lagatar, hazaar-bar
Main dadaon ka dada hoon aur gundo ka gunda hoon  yeh sansar meri dharti aur main iska jhanda hoon
Yeh shehar jahanum hai, narak hai aur hum hai is shehar ka Yamraj  kisko, kahan aur kab maarna hai  yeh hum decide karoonga
