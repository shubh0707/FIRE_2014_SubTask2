Aapka kanoon kisi insaan ko ek hi jurm ke liye do baar sazaa nahi de sakta
Allah re jism-e-yaar ki khoobi ke khud-ba-khud  rangeeniyon mein doob gaya pairahan tamaam
Ek maut tere gunaahon ke sazaa ke liye kaafi nahi
Aaj tak woh rassi hi nahi batti gayi  joh mujhe phansi laga sake
Maut se kiski rishteydari hai  aaj hamari kal tumhari baari hai
Pyar bhari zindagi ka ek pal  bina pyar ki sadiyon se kai bada hota hai
Maine usse maara bhi nahi  aur maar bhi dala
Kanoon itna maila hai  ki usse apne haath mein lekar main apna haath ganda karna chahta bhi nahi
