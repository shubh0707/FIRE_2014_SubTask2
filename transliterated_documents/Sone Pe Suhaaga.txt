Pehle khoon ke baad aadmi kanoon se darta hai  lekin do-char khoon karne ke baad kanoon aadmi se darta hai
Joh kam bolte hai, woh aksar khatarnak hote hai  is liye chalak aadmi ko khamosh dushman se hamesha hoshiyar rehna chahiye
Baazuon ki taqat toh uparwale ki den hai
Bhagwan ke prem aur vishvas ka ek hi saboot hai duniya mein  joh maa ke dil mein mamta bankar palta rehta hai
Aasoon gareeb ki dil ki tijori se nikalne waale keemti moti hote hai
Har insaan apne karmon se pehchana jaata hai  apne baap ke karmon se nahi
Izzat toh daulat walon ki hoti hai  bhikhariyon ki toh izzat ko bhi duniya nafrat ki nazar se dekhti hai
Naam lene se umar lambi nahi hoti  umar lambi hoti hai mehnat se, mushaqat se
