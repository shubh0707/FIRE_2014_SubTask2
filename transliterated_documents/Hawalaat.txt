Aurat ke daman par agar ek baar daag lag jaata hai  toh Ganga ka pavitra jal bhi usse nahi dho sakta
Mangal ka rasta joh rokta hai  Mangal uske liye maut ka darwaza khol deta hai
Chalo dhabe mein sab kuch milega  mutton kadi, alu badi, maa ki daal raita naal, fry machhi bachut achchi, shami kabab lajawab, paneer palak  chalo balak
Mangal ke samne joh zabaan chalata hai  Mangal uski taqdeer ki linen mitta deta hai
