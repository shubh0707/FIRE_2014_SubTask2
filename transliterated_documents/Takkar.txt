Udaasi ka sirf ek sur hota hai, jisse pakadkar main jeeti rahi hoon  aur gusse ka bhi ek hi sur hota hai, jisse pakadkar tum jee rahe ho  magar zindagi mein saat sur hote hai  kya hum in saaton suron ko pakadkar nahi chal sakte
Aap hasti hai  toh ghungroo bajhte hai
Bahut auratein dekhi jinke liye jaan di jaa sakti hai  pehli baar ek aaisi ladki dekhi jiske liye jaan li jaa sakti hai
Tum agar saath ho toh kisi cheez se darr nahi lagta  darrta hoon toh sirf tumhe khone se
Nafrat ki is duniya mein mohabbat shayad sabse bada jurm hai
Bhayanak sapno ko bhool jaana  achchi baat nahi
Seedhe ghar jaoge yah  haspatal hokar jaoge
Mard ki zillat aurat ke balatkaar se zyada sharamnak hoti hai
Dimaag kharch karne se  dimaag kam nahi hota
Halaat insaan ko shaitaan bana deta hai  magar devta nahi
Police na kisise mohabbat karti hai, na nafrat karti hai  hum sirf duty karte hai
Bachchon mein zid achchi lagti hai, auraton mein shaandar lagti hai, badshahon mein bereham lagti hai  magar iski yeh zid kaisi hai reh?
Dard itna meetha bhi ho sakta hai  yeh aaj pata chala
