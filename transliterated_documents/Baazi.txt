Joh doosro ko tapkane ki himmat rakhte hai  un mein khud bhi tapakne ka kaleja rehta hai
Kal tak main supari leta tha doosro ko khatam karne ke liye  lekin aaj maine apne aap ko supari di hai tujhe khatam karne ke liye
Dole dole mera bhi dil dole  isse pehle ki meri joote bole  tu patli gali hole
Agar shatranj ki baazi jeetni ho  toh usse dus dimaag se nahi, ek dimaag se khelna chahiye
Tumhare jism mein main itne chhed kar doonga  ki marne ke baad bhi saale basuri ki tarah bajhte rahoge
Insaan jhoot bol sakta hai  lekin uske ungliyon ke nishaan jhoot nahi bol sakte
