Kapde badalne se aadmi ki asliyat nahi badal jaati
Aap ek achche aadmi hai  aur ek achche aadmi ke haath mein sharaab ki bottal achchi nahi lagti
Waqt se pehle aur naseeb ke baad koi cheez agar tere pass dene ko ho  toh mujhe de dena
Zindagi insaan ko laati hai, maut le jaati hai  yeh sharaab beech mein kahan aati hai
Apne desh mein ladkiyan kapde utaarkar nahi  balki pehenkar khoobsurat lagti hai
Jiske sar pe tujh jaise dost ki dosti ka saaya ho  uske liye bankar aayi maut, uske dushmano ki maut ban jaati hai
Hum apne dhande mein jis kisike saath taluk rakhte hai  uski agli, pichli, sari zindagi ka hisaab kitaab apni kitaab mein rakh lete hai
