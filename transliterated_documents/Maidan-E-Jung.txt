Darye us bhagwan se  sabse badi goli hoti hai uske paas joh bewaqt chalti hai
Zindagi ek paheli hai  kabhi dushman, kabhi saheli hai
Aaj ke is kalyug mein  tumhara bhagwan bhi is shaitan se darta hai
Kharbuja, kharbuje ko dekh kar rang badalta hai
Jung ke maidan mein ladne wala har sipahi barabar hota hai
Sher ke shikaar ki tamanna uman har shikari karta hai  magar har shikari ki yeh tamanna puri nahi hoti
Chot lagegi toh hi mazboot ho jaoge
Iske tukde tukde karke poore gaon mein prasad ki tarah baant do
Shikast humne kabhi dekhi nahi  jeet-e-jee hum kabhi mare nahi
Hamare yahan toh yamdoot bhi hamari ijaazat ke bina nahi aa sakta
Jis saanp ko barso doodh pilaya  ussi ne khandan ki izzat ko dasa
Doobi hui kashti se kinara poochte ho, bete?
Zalalat ka jo kaanta mere dil mein gad gaya hai  uski chubhan mujhe zindagi bhar tadpaati rahegi
Humne tumhe paal poskar is din ke liye bada nahi kiya tha  ki humse sawal jawab karne ki jurrat karo
