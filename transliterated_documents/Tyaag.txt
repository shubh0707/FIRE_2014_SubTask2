Arre kha le na yaar, kha le  khali pet toh bhagwan ka naam bhi pet mein dard karta hai
Insaan marta aur jeeta sirf apne khayalon mein hai  varna maut jaisi toh koi cheez hi nahi hai
Purani zameen, purana makaan aur purana dost  jitna zyada purana ho jaye  utna hi zyada yaad aata hai
Main zindagi mein kadve se kadva sach bardaash kar sakta hoon  lekin meethe se meetha jhoot nahi
Duniya mein kuch kaam aaise hote hai jinhe karne ke liye bhagwan insaan banata hai  aur woh insaan utne hi bade hote hai, jitna bada bhagwan khud hota hai
Waqt padne par aadmi jhoot bhi sach ki tarah bolta hai
Aadmi bure se bure aadmi ka yakeen kar sakta hai  lekin aurat zaat par bharosa nahi kar sakta
Aadmi har darr se bhaag sakta hai  lekin apne aap se nahi
