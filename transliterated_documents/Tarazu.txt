Buraiyon se ladte ladte joh log mar jaate hai  unki maut ko maut nahi  shahadat kaha jaata hai
Ek ladka, ek ladki, chota sa ghar pyara pyara  yahi sandesh de raha hai desh hamara
Tu kanoon ko khareed sakta hai, adalaton ko khareed sakta hai  lekin bhagwan ke us insaaf ko nahi khareed sakta
Yeh adalat hai na, yeh rishton aur jazbaton se nahi chalti  adalat chalti hai sabooton se, adalat chalti hai gawahon se
