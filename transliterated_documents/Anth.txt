Jab samne waala garam hota hai na  toh khud ko naram rehne ka
Ab tak tera muqabla buzdilon se hua hai  jab samna is mard se hoga na  toh teri yeh sher banne ki galatphami door ho jayegi
Ab tak tum is vardi ki ahmiyat ko samajh nahi paye ho  kyun ki tum ek aaise dimak ho jisne is police station ki deewaron ko itna khokla kar diya hai  ki koi mamuli sa ladka isse todh sakta hai
Padhe likhe logon ke saath mein hai na, kabhi likha padhi ka kaam nahi karna chahiye
Jurm ki chataan ko kranti ki lehren takra takrakar todh deti hai
Duaon se swarg aur narak ke faisle hote hai  kanoon ke nahi
Paap ka ghinauna rang main teri aankhon mein dekh raha hoon  yeh rang maut ka elaan hai  tu hi nahi, tujh jaise har paapi ka anth ab bahut hi nazdeek hai
