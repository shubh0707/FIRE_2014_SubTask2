Raaton ko jaagna meri kismat ban chuki hai
Dard ki dawa na ho  toh dard ko hi dawa samajh lena chahiye
Jab tum mujhe apna kehte ho  apne pe guroor aa jaata hai
Jeevan ke kis modh pe, kab koi mil jaaye  kaun keh sakta hai?
Pyar hawa ka ek jhonka hai  joh sab udakar le jaata hai
Hum Delhi waale mulk ke saath saath  dil pe bhi hukumat karna jaante hai
