Kanoon ki talwar se  kanoon ka hi khoon kiya hai tumne
Shatranj ke mohre wahi hai  magar baazi palat chuki hai
Mujhe tumhari maut nahin, zindagi chahiye
Jiske paas koi saboot yah gawah nah ho  kya woh nirdosh nahin hota?
Kya main ek phool ki tarah khoobsurat ladki ko ek  ladki ki tarah khoobsurat phool de sakta hoon
Jab gareeb ka dil roh uthta hai  toh apne hosh kho beheta hai
