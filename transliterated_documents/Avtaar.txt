Retire toh aadmi ko us din hona chahiye  jab upar waale ka bulava aa jaye
Bada aadmi toh woh hota hai joh doosron ko chota nahi samajhta
Yaaron utho, chalo, bhaago, daudo  marne se pehle jeena na chhodo
Kisi ka fayda, kisi ka nuksaan  kisi ka nuksaan, kisi ka fayda  issi ka naam duniya hai
Kaun kiske kareeb hoga, apna apna naseeb hoga  yaad karega jab azeez koi, dard dil ke kareeb hoga
Is kambakht neend ko kya hua ki aati hi nahi  aur tumhari yaad hai ki jaati hi nahi
Daulat se zindagi ka har jazba, har lamha khareeda ja sakta hai
Kya husn ne samjha hai, kya ishq ne jaana hai  hum khaak nasheeno ki thokar mein zamana hai
Pachtave ke ek aasoon se  saare gunaah bhagwan bhi maaf kar deta hai
Daulat libaas badal sakti hai  jism ko nahi badal sakti
Mazaa toh jab hai aa jaye kahin unki tabhiyat bhi  zara woh bhi toh jaane karvatein kyun kar badalte hai
