Har kamyab insaan ke dushman zyada aur dost kam hote hai
Rupaiyon mein woh taqat hai  ki imaandar se imaandar insaan ki bhi neeyat bighadte derr nahi lagti
Jab jab dharm ki haani aur adharm ki vriddhi hoti hai  tab tab swayam Sri Krishna ji kisi bhi roop mein aakar  dushton ka naash aur sajjanon ki rashka karte hai
Tum toh aaise zaleel insaan ho  ki tumhe agar munafa nazar aaye toh dharti maa toh kya  tum toh apni sagi maa ko bhi bech doge
Jumma aaye, jumma jaye  lekin is chumme ki nishani kabhi na mitne paye
