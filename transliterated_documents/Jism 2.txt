Us swarg se kya lena jismein tum nahin  aur us nark se kya darna jismein tum saath ho
Pyar ishq mohabbat  duniya ka sabse aakhri aur khatarnak nasha hai
Joh pagal na karde woh ishq hi kya
Yeh duniya pyar ki sirf baatein karti hai  chalti jung pe hai
Har jism ki ek expiry date hoti hai
Itni shiddat se wahi nafrat kar sakta hai  jisne mohabbat bhi itni shiddat se ki ho
Sirf ek hi shikayat hai apne khoon ke rang se  iska rang mere ehsaas ki tarah gehra nahin
