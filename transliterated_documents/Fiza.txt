Jin gharon par Allah meherbaan hota hai  wahan betiyan paida hoti hai
Harra rang aur bhagwa rang, green and saffron  Hindustan ke jhande ka ek hi hissa hai  usse ek hissa hi rehna chahiye  poora jhanda banne ki koshish nahi karni chahiye
Jeena koi majboori nahi, ek maqsad hai
Chhot toh budhape ki hoti hai  ek baar lag jaye, toh dard marte dam tak hota hai
Dard jitna zyada hota hai  cheenkh utni hi lambi hoti hai
Hasee nahi hai toh is duniya mein kuch bhi nahi hai  haste raho, haste raho, haste raho
Asli imaan sirf mohabbat hai
Kalam ki taqat talwaar se zyada hoti hai
Insaaniyat is duniya se bas laapata si ho gayi hai
Maa ki bad-dua bhi dua bankar lagti hai
Bimaari ko chupane se rog kam nahi hota  badh jaata hai
Duniya mein saharon ki sabhi ko zaroorat hoti hai  kisi ke pyar se badkar duniya mein koi sahara nahi hota
