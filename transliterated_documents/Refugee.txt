Shaadi ke bagair mohabbat gunaah hai
Janwaro pe bharosa kiya ja sakta hai  insaano par nahi
Tumne mujhe zindagi ke woh haseen khwab kyun dikhaye  jiske tootne par mere andhar ka insaan hi toot gaya
School kabhi gaya nahi  par zindagi har roz ek naya sabak sikhati hai
Mohabbat karne waale  kabhi mohabbat ke dushman nahi hote
Insaan itna ghatiya janwar hai  ki waqt padhne par khuda ki zaat se bhi inkaar karde
Insaan duniya ka sabse ghatiya janwar hai
Haddiyan toh kabhi na kabhi judh hi jayengi  magar mere andhar joh kuch bhi toota hai na  woh kabhi nahi judhega
Main awaara aur badmaash bardaasht kar sakta hoon  lekin gaddar kabhi nahi
Tere huzoor se sab sharmsar hokar chale  shikar karne ko aaye the, shikaar hokar chale
Fauji ka ek haath pada na  toh che foot ka aadmi dedh foot ka hokar reh jaata hai
Zindagi bas ek safar hai  sacha ghar toh sabka ek hi hai  upar waale ka
Gehre pani mein utarne ke baad  doobne se ghabrana bekaar hai
Gham aur thakaan ka mel  jaanlewa hota hai
Dosti ki tarah dushmani bhi ek tarfa nahi hoti  dono taraf se hoti hai
Aadmi jab bahut khush hota hai  roh deta hai
