Main hoon Nandu sabka bandhu
Jis saanp ko main sari zindagi apna khoon pilata raha  us saanp ne aaj mujhko dassa hai
Samajhta nahi hai yaar
Kar bhala toh ho bhala
Itna zoor se laafa marke mooh laal kar doonga  ki zindagi bhar yeh paan khane ki zaroorat nahi padegi
Agar mere maa baap ko kuch hua na  kaat daloonga
Joh bhi pyar se mila hum us hi ke ho liye  jahan par khatiya mili hum wahin pe soo liye
Ab samajh mein aaya upar waale ka yeh khel  jab woh aulaad deta hai  toh dil mein uske liye dard bhi apne aap hi de deta hai
