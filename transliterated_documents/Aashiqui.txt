Ab mujhe tumhe pyar karne se koi nahi rok sakta  tum bhi nahi
Tumhara naam maine sirf apne jism par hi nahi  apne dil, apne dimag aur apni aatma par bhi gaad diya hai
Koi bhi rishta kitna bhi mahaan, kitna bhi bada kyun na ho  uske khatam ho jane se zindagi khatam nahi hoti
Sansar mein agar koi cheez logon ko zinda rakhti hai na  toh woh pyar hai, pyar
Rishta wahi safal ho sakta hai  jisme do logon ke beech barabari ho
Bahar ki duniya mein kadam rakhte hi tumko pata chal jayega  ki zindagi kis berehami se mushkilon ka samna karati hai
Ek chota aadmi apne ochepan ka saboot tab deta hai  jab woh bewajah kisi ki kabiliyat pe shak karta hai
