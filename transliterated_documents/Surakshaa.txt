Yeh crorepati hai, yeh arabpati hai  magar aaise rehte hai jaise chai ki patti hai
Main jab bhi koi khel khelta hoon  hamesha uska ek patta bahar chhodkar aata hoon
Jin logon ki zindagi ka koi maqsad hota hai  unhe aurat ki duniya se door rehna chahiye
Hamare is kaale dhande mein zindagi aur maut ka khel toh chalta hi rehta hai
