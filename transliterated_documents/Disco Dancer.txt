Zakhm toh bhar jaate hai  lekin nishaan reh jaate hai
Kal ka intezaar karna seekho  kal parso nahin aayega  kal hi aayega
Who din toh meri atma par ek taaza zakhm ki tarah zinda hai
Sharif ghar ki ladkiyan  apne maa baap ki izaazat ke bagair khwab tak nahin dekhti
Maarne waale ka haath pakad sakte hai  bolne waale ki zubaan nahin
