Meri zabaan se ughla hua zehar bhi amrut ban jaata hai
Insaan jab aadmiyon se mayoos ho jaata hai  toh phir kutte aur billiyon se pyar karne lagta hai
Har saas yeh bhool jaati hai ki woh bhi kabhi bahu thi  aur har bahu yeh bhool jaati hai ki usse bhi ek din kisi ki saas banna hai
Zindagi bhar nahi bhoolegi woh barsaat ki raat  maine jab pakda tha kambakht teri saas ka haath
Hai Ram kya waqt aa gaya hai  maa ki shiksha ka amrut bhi beti ko zehar lag raha hai
Kavita toh atma ki khuraakh hoti hai  achcha sher sunne ya kehne se dil aur dimaag saaf ho jaate hai
Maya Devi toh meri bachpan ki saheli hai  hum ek doosre ke naakhun tak pehchante hai
Mohabbat ek tam-tam hai joh daudai nahi jaati  bhadak uthti hai apne aap hi, bhadkai nahi jaati
Duniya mein kisi ko gaali dena shayad paap na hoga  lekin gareeb ko baar baar gareeb aur mohtaj ko baar baar mohtaj kehne se bada kukarma aur koi nahi hai
Purani haddiyan badi mazboot hoti hai
Mohabbat ko mile aasoon wahan se  sitare banke aate hai jahan se
Agar aadmi ko galati karne ka adhikar hai  toh galati karke pachtane ka haq bhi hai
Nagan khubsoorat hoti hai  par das bhi deti hai
Saas kisi ki bhi ho  sab ek jaisi hoti hai
Hum apne pote ka dada banenge  tabla, sarangi aur baja banenge
Meri chaati par aakar toh saanp bhi rassi ban jaata hai
Khoob phulegi phalegi beti Harparsad ki  baap ne toh karke shaadi zindagi barbaad ki
