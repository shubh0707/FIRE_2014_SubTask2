Zinda rehne ke liye apne sivah kisi aur ka hona bilkul zaroori nahi hai  aadmi jinke saath jeena chahta hai woh unke bagair bhi jee sakta hai
Bus mein tel jalta hai aur taange mein janavar ka khoon jalta hai
Pyar ka mazaa saath marne mein nahi  saath jeene mein hai
Yeh toh anyay hai, ghor anyay  aur main har anyay ka virodh karunga
Kanoon kehta hai ki karz agar purana ho jaye  toh usse chukane ki koi zaroorat nahi
