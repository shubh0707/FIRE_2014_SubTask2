Meri duniya mein mere paas khone ke liye kuch nahi hai  lekin tumhari duniya mein tumhare paas khone ke liye bahut kuch hai
Pyar karna, izhaar karna bahut hi aasaan hai  lekin usko nibhana bahut mushkil
Mohabbat karne waalon ke maula saath hai  aye dushman teri kya taqat, teri kya aukaat hai
Kuch bhi nahi kuch nahi hai  bin tere kuch nahi hai
Is duniya ke safar mein doston ke saath saath  ek humsafar ki bhi zaroorat hoti hai
Baahon mein Tina, kamar mein Meena, kandhon mein Leena aur sar par Henna  yaar tune toh apne pyar ka ek Jumbo circus banakar rakha hai
Jiske paas ho pyar ki amanat  ab Allah karega uski hifazat
