Bachche bado ko bhool jaaye par bade bachchon ko nahi bhoolte
Iss duniya ki gaadi baimaani aur rishwat ke paiyon par chal rahi hai
Sangeet woh daulat hai jisse duniya ka koi chor chura nahi sakta, koi daaku loot nahi sakta  sangeet woh jadoo hai jiske samne duniya ka har jadoo beqaboo ho jaata hai
Paise mein badi taqat hoti hai  jinke paas paisa nahi hai na, unse toh unke apne bhi nazar pher lete hai  aur jinke paas paisa hai, unke gair tak apne ho jaate hai
