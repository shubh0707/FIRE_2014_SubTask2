Meri zindagi mein koi aur aa gaya hai  ek sailaab, ek toofan ki tarah aur baha le gaya mujhe
Tumhe koi chahe yeh toh bahut hi mamuli si baat hai  magar tum kisi ko is tarah tootkar chaho bahut badi baat hai
Kiya hai ishq toh har imtihaan se guzrenge  tumhare vaaste har intehaan se guzrenge
Us waqt par joh maine sahi samjha maine kiya  lekin aaj joh hone ja raha hai woh bilkul galat hai
Auratein apne pati ko apna bhagwan maanti hai  aur main woh khushnaseeb hoon joh apne bhagwan ko apna pati banane ja rahi hoon
Us aadmi ka sab kuch ho kar bhi kuch nahi ho sakta  jiska ghar nahi bana hota
