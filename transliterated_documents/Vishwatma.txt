Ajgar kisse, kab aur kahan nighal jaata hai  yeh toh marne waale ko bhi pata nahin chalta
Meri shaadi ho chuki hai, magar maut se
Arre yeh lo  aa gaye kalyug ke kanhaiya
Tumhare aur maut ke beech  meri pistol se nikli sirf ek goli ka faasla reh gaya hai
Main sarkari afsar hoon  farz mera dharm hai
Jab bhi hum koi khel khelte hai  toh saare mohron ko apni ungliyon par nachate hai
Aaj hoga mere aakhri dushman ka khaatma  aur phir kehlaunga main is duniya ka vishwatma
Ajgar apne shikaar ko das kar nahi maarta  balki usse apne lapet mein jakadkar uski haddiyon ke tukde tukde kar deta hai  aur uski saanson ka gubaar nikal deta hai
Vishwa mein unhi atmaon ko puja jaata hai  joh shanti ke marg par chalte hai
Ek kaayar bete ka pita kehlane se kahi behtar hai  ki ek bahadur shaheed ka baap kehlaon
Thappad tumhare mooh par pada hai  aur nishaan mere gaal par chhape hai
