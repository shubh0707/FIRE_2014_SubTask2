Tum shayad mere bagair reh sakte ho  magar main tumhare bagair na reh sakti hoon, na jee sakti hoon  haan mar sakti hoon
Keechad aadmi par girre yah aadmi keechad par  dono suraton mein daag toh aadmi par hi lagta hai
Joh toofan mein palta hai  wohi duniya badalta hai
Aashirwad toh main usse aaisa doonga  ki mera aashirwad uske liye hi nahi  balki uske khandan ke liye abhishap banke reh jayega
Jaagti hui aankhon ka khwab kabhi poora nahi hota
