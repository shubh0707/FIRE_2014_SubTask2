Khushi toh naam hai tumse nazar milane ka  nazar milana ek bahana hai dil lagane ka
Joh aurat kisi ke pyar ki kasam kha chuki ho  woh paraye mard ke haath lagne se hi mar jaati hai
Har smuggler desh ka gaddar hota hai  aur charas ka smuggler apne desh ka hi nahi  sari insaaniyat ka dushman hota hai
Lalach insaan ko ek pagal kutta bana deti hai  joh apne malik ko bhi kaat leta hai
Mere dil mein toh ek jwalamukhi hai jiske dhadakte sholon mein  in logon ko zinda jala dene ko jee chahta hai
Pyar ek panchi hai joh aasman ki bulandiyon tak udhna chahta hai  magar insaan ke farz aur majbooriyan usse dharti se door nahi jaane deti
Tum asteen ke woh saanp nikle jiska kaata pani tak nahi maangta
When I kill them  they stay dead
