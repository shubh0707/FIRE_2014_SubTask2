Manushya ke jeevan mein sangeet ka staan asadharan hai
Shaadi ke mandap mein pallu se ghaant baandhane waala pati nahi hota  balki patni ki zindagi ki guthiyan suljhane waala pati hota hai  pavitra agni ke gird phere lene waala pati nahi hota  balki patni ke jeevan ko har aag se bachane waala pati hota hai
Ek aurat apne pati ki gareebi mein khush reh sakti hai  uske aeb seh sakti hai  lekin uski kayarta nahi seh sakti
Jis desh ko hamare buzurgon ne apne pranon ki aahuti dekar azaad karaya  ussi desh mein in kayaron ki fauj pal rahi hai
Unke dekhe se joh aati hai chehre pe raunak  woh samajhte hai beemar ka haal achcha hai
