Noton ka malik wahi hota hai  joh unhe apni jeb mein rakhta hai
Hamare dhande mein maafi nahi chalti  sirf luck chalti hai
Bichhoo se zyada khatarnak hoon main  aur saanp se zyada zehereela
Tum dushmano ko taane marte ho  main dushmano ko goli marta hoon
Ek baar apne jism par hamla bardaash kar sakta hoon magar  mere dil par joh hamla karega, main usse kabhi maaf nahi karoonga
Main aate ki roti khata hoon  aur tum gobar ki roti khate ho
Tumne gareeb dekhe honge  gareebi nahi
Agar koi gareeb ladki apni izzat bechna na chahe  toh duniya ka koi bhi amir uski izzat khareed nahi sakta
Jab hum offer karte hai  toh koi hum mein inkaar bhi nahi karta
Sarr par croreron ke heere rakhne se  aadmi akalmand nahi ho jaata
