Kabhi aadmi leke nikalta hai, kabhi kutte leke  akele nikla kar  mard hone ka ehsaas ho jayega
Isse bachane mein mera koi fayda nahi  aur tujhe todh daalne mein mera koi nuksaan nahi
Main baat se zaat jaan jaata hoon  aur harkato se aukaad
Ladki ka peecha chhod de varna tere itne aadmi marenge  ki tujh jaisa tenth fail lashein bhi nahi gin payega
Logon ke toh kapde istri kiye hue hote hai  tumhara toh chehra hi istri kiya hua hai
Joh apne peeche nishaan chhodte hai  aksar unka naam aur nishaan mitt jaata hai
Joh log sirf tamasha dekhte hai  unka aksar tamasha ban jaata hai
Expiry date aa gayi hai teri
Jab sau kamine marre honge na  tab bhagwan ne tujhe unki kami poori karne ke liye bheja hoga
Assi phutenge nahi  assi kutenge!
Banda jitna teda haunda hai na  usko seedha karke maarne mein utna hi maaza aata hai
