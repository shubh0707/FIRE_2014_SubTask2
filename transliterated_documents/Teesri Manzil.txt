Duniya mein bahut si aaisi baatein hoti hai joh namumkin nazar aati hai  lekin agar insaan himaat se kaam kare aur woh sachcha hai  toh jeet ussi ki hoti hai
Milap ki teen manzilen hoti hai  pehli manzil nafrat aur ladai  doosri manzil dosti aur safai  teesri manzil pyar aur sagai
Jab koi nadaan ladki pyar ki manzilon se guzar jaati hai  apni izzat aur abroo kho beheti hai  toh anjaam zindagi nahi, maut hota hai
