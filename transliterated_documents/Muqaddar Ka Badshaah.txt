Upar waale ne yeh dil patthar ka banaya hai  isse shock toh lag sakta hai, lekin kabhi stop nahi ho sakta
Muqaddar ke badshah ke liye kuch bhi namumkin nahi hai
Ek baap das bachche paida kar sakta hai  lekin das bachche milkar ek baap nahi bana sakte
Joh rishta swarth ke dhaago se bandha ho  uske toot jaane mein koi harj nahi
Naye jooton ki tarah shuru mein naye naye afsar bhi kaatte hai
Ek patni apni pati ke liye apne armaano ka khoon toh kar sakti hai  lekin ek maa apni aulad ki khushi ke liye sari duniya se takra sakti hai
Jab aulad ki zindagi ka sawaal ho  toh maa din aur raat nahi dekhti
