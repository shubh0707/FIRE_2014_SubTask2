Shakaal jab baazi khelta hai  toh jitne patte uske haath mein hote hai  utne hi uski aasteen mein
Main apne plan khud banata hoon
In kandhon par wahi sar hai jisne katna seekha hai  jhukna nahi
Jab dil bahut kuch kehne ko chahe aur zubaan saath na de  toh yeh mohabbat ki pehli seedi hoti hai
Main tere napaak khoon se haath nahi rangoonga
Pilana bhi farz tha toh kuch bhi pila diya hota  sharaab kam thi toh pani mila diya hota
Joh apni jaan par khelte hai  woh apni manzil par khud pahunch jaate hai
