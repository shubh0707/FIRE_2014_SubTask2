De shiva var mohe, shubh karman se kabhoo na hatton  na daroon bairi sauh jab chahe ladoon, nishchay kar apni jeet karoon
Apna vasiyat nama banakar rakho  kyun ki maut kisi bhi waqt tumhare ghar ka darwaza khatkhata sakti hai
Insaan ka itna sach bolna bhi acha nahi  ki uski zubaan ko rokne ke liye uski saanson ko bandh karna pade
