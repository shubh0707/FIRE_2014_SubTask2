Kehte hai pyar ki shuruvat pehle na se hoti hai  phir haan tak pahunchti hai
Ghar bada ho ya chota  ghar mein rehne waalo ka dil bada hona chahiye
Yeh baat itni important nahi hai ke hum kisse pyar kare  balke yeh baat zyada important hai ke humse kaun pyar karta hai
Mere nazar mein saree ki neeche dikhne waali pairon ki halki si jhalak  skirt ki neeche dikhne waali poori nangi taango se kai zyada dilkash hai
Duniya mein koi bhi anaath nahi hota  har ek ko apna sukh dukh baatne ke liye koi na koi saathi mil hi jaata hai
Hamesha yahi hota hai ki pyar aankhon se shuru hokar dil mein utarta hai  lekin hamara pyar dil se shuru hokar aankhon tak pohchega
Pyar dimaag se nahi, dil se kiya jaata hai
Har cheez pehli baar bilkul nayi hoti hai  aur phir dheere dheere uski aadat ho jaati hai
Aksar logon ki zindagi mein mauka darwaza khat-khata hai  lekin aapki zindagi mein, mauka dil ko khat-khata raha hai
Mera pyar hi mera ghar hai, meri chahat hi meri car hai aur meri pavitrata hi mera bank balance hai
Jo doosro ko napasand hai, woh mujhe pasand hai
