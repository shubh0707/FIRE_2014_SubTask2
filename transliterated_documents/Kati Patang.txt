Apnepan ke liye kisiko apna banana zaroori nahi  jisse bhi apnapan mil jaye wohi apna hai
Kavita se mann ko behlaya ja sakta hai  samjhaya nahi ja sakta
Kailash khud nahi sochta  doosro ko majboor karta hai sochne ke liye
Na koi tarang hai, na koi umang hai  meri zindagi bhi kya ek kati patang hai
Aandhi mein udhte hue patte ko kya malum  usse kahan se kahan bhatakna hoga
Main joh aag lagata hoon  usse bhujana bhi jaanta hoon
Agar sharaab dukhon ko mitta sakti  toh har aadmi sharaabi ban jata
Pyar apni jagah hai, daulat apni jagah hai  zindagi ke liye dono zaroori hai
Jab ghar ki jawaan bahu bahar rang raliyan manati phire  toh budhe sasur ko neend kaise aa sakti hai
Ghar mein devi jaisi bahu hai, toh phir tirth yatra ki kya zaroorat?
Bhagwan ki kaisi anokhi leela hai  kabhi sab kuch cheen leta hai  kabhi bahut kuch de deta hai
