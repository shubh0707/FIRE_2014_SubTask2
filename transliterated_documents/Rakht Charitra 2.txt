Ab mere paas sirf badla hai
Ateeth ko sirf yaad rakho  vartamaan ko parkho  aur bhavishya ka chinta karo
Main marne se nahi  usse maare bina marne se darta hoon
Main maarne ko tayaar hoon  aur uske liye marne ko bhi tayaar hoon
Bade aadmi ko maarne waala aur bhi bada ban jaata hai
Todne se nahi  dushmano ko jodne se taqat banti hai
Darr ko badla mein badal jaane ke liye  waqt nahi lagta
Woh mujhe maarna chahta hai  aur mujhe usse maarna padega
Pehle usse harana hai  aur phir maarna hai
Maut sirf shareer ki hoti hai  sooch ki nahi
Har koi joh uski madad kare ya madad karne ki sooche bhi  udhaa do
Meri taqat bano  kamzoori nahi
Mera raasta ab sirf tere raaste mein aana hai
Makaan banao  baatein nahi
