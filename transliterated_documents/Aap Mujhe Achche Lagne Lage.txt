Kehte hai ki jab bachche ke zid baap poori na kar sake  toh woh apni maa ke paas chala jaata hai
Main tumhara haath kabhi nahi chhodunga  aaj nahi, abhi nahi, kabhi nahi
Hamare dhande mein hum doston se zyada dushmano ki khabar rakhte hai
Aap haath uthayen ya haath phailayen  main apna pyar aapki jholi mein nahi daal sakta
Pyar mein jeet ussi ki hoti hai  joh raaste mein aane waali har deewar ko gira de
Pathan kabhi burkhe ke peeche chip kar vaar nahi karta
Apni beti ko toh main sirf is desh se door bhej raha hoon  magar aaj ke baad Sapna ka naam bhi teri zubaan pe aaya  toh main tujhe zindagi se door bhej doonga
Hamare dhande mein kisi ek ki kamzori  doosre ki taqat ban jaati hai
