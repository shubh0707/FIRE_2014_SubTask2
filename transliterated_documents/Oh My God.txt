Main Krishna hoon isliye chamatkar karta hoon  chamatkar karta hoon isliye Krishna nahin hoon
Jaise ek bakht bhagwan ke bina adhoora hota hai  waise hi bhagwan bhi ek sachche bakht ke bina adhoora hota hai
Jahan dharm hai na, wahan satya ke liye jagah nahi hai  aur jahan satya hai truth hai, wahan dharm ki zaroorat hi nahi hai
Maine sirf insaan banaya  aur insaan ne yeh jaat, paat, dharm, mazhab ka dhanda shuru kiya
Mazhab insaanon ke liye banta hai  mazhab ke liye insaan nahi bante
Main sab dharmon ka aadhar karta hoon  aur kisi bhi dharm mein maanta nahin
Sachchi baat hoti hai na woh sharaab se bhi zyada kadvi lagti hai
