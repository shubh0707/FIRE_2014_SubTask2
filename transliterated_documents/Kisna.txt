Nirdosh ladki ki raksha karna  bhagwan ka adhar karna hi nahi  purush ka pehla karam bhi hai
Sacha pyar hamesha vishvas, sabra aur tyag se hi panapta hai
Anyaay ke khilaf har yudh ek nyaay hai
Aatma ka prem shareer ke prem se bahut ooncha hota hai
Har dharam ka ek hi matlab hota hai  prem aur shanti ke raaste par chalna
Log badle, desh badle, tareekh badli  nahi badli toh ek insaan ki sachi mohabbat
