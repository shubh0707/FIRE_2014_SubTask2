Kal uske jism ki har haddi toot chuki hogi
Usse yeh kaho ki un raasto par chalna chhod de  jahan par hamari hone waali bahu chalti hai
Katal karna hamara dhanda hai  aur yeh kaam bhi hum muft nahi karte
Gussa ek bahut badi daulat hoti hai  usse bhi sooch samajhkar karch karna chahiye
Khwab aur hakikat mein jab bhi jung hoti hai  khwab haar jaata hai
Hum uski woh beizzati karenge, woh beizzati karenge  ki saale ki aane waali saath nasale beizzat pehda hongi
Meri aadat hai, main jis cheez ko ek baar pasand karta hoon  usse yah toh paa leta hoon  yah usse is layak nahi chhodta ki woh kisi aur ke layak rahe
Is duniya mein sharifon ki bahumat hai  lekin yeh sharif kuch hi gundon se sar jhukaye darr darr ke jeete hai
Abhi toh tamasha hoga  bahut bada tamasha hoga
Aayenda uske aas paas bhi dhikai diya  toh itni zor se maroonga ki thook bhi nahi ugal sakega
