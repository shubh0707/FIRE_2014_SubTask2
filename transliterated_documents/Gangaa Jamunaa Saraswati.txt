Maut apni jagah aur waqt likhakar aati hai
Insaan ka jab waqt pura ho jaye  toh kadam khud ba khud wahan pahuncha dete hai  jahan maut bahen phalye uska intezar kar rahi ho
Kuch badnaseeb musafir aaisi bhi hote hai  jinki manzil toh samne hoti hai lekin rasta hi khatam ho jaata hai
Agar tujh jaise bhai aur pehda ho jaye  toh is duniya se Rakhi ka tyohar mitt jayega
Tawaif ka muqaddar  kothe se shuru hokar kothe pe hi khatam hota hai
Jab jab is dharti par Kans ne janam liya hai  uska sar kuchalne ke liye Krishna bhi pehda hota raha hai
Shankar jab gale se gaa sakta hai  kahin khopdi ghumi na  toh dushmanon ko jalakar khaak bhi kar sakta hai
Jisse hum khareed nahi sakte  usse hum khatam kar dete hai
