Har tawaif dalle se zyada pyar ke shabd se nafrat karti hai
Bachchon ke liye kitaabein likhna bahut asaan hai  magar unke liye loriyan gaana bahut mushkil hai
Sar par chhat ho na ho  paon ke neeche ek zameen ka tudka toh chahiye
Sharton par pyar nahi hota
Pyar bheek se nahi milta
Haar tab hoti hai jab haar maan li jaye
Aisa hamesha zaroori nahi hai ki jis raaste par maa baap chale  ussi raaste par aulad bhi chale
Ladki aisi honi chahiye  thodi si namkeen, thodi si khatti, thodi si meethi  rang aisa ki chandni ki ujaale mein jaise thoda sa shaam ka andhera shamil ho
Duniya mein tu aayi hai ashk peene ke liye  muskurana hi padega tujhe jeene ke liye
Jitna bada aadmi, utna hi lamba intezaar
Khata ek pal ki hoti hai  lekin uski sazaa saalon saal tak bhugatni padti hai
Jhoot ka bhi rajneeti mein ek khaas maqam hai
Dalle aur pille ke saath ek jaisa suluk karna chahiye  agar baat se na maane, toh laat maarni chahiye
Ishq ke saamne zamane waale haarte aaye hai
Jalan ka bhi mohabbat mein ek khaas maqam hai
Khilono aur tohfo ki bachchon ko itni bhook nahi hoti jitni maa baap ke pyar ki
Jaane kya badalon ke darmiyan saazish hui  uska ghar mitti ka tha, uske hi ghar baarish hui
Sharifon ka libaaz pehenne se  awargi sharafat nahi ban jaati
Unke dekhne se joh aa jaati hai chehre pe raunak  woh samajhte hai bimaar ka haal achcha hai
Jawani bhi aisi hi car hai jis mein brake nahi hota
Halaat ki majboori se agar insaan keechad mein gir jaaye  toh uska khoon ganda nahi ho jaata
Yaadon ka insaan ki zindagi mein ek khaas maqam hai
Yeh zindagi, milna bichadna  sab lakeeron ka khel hai
Chor jab maal pakadta hai, toh usse tijori thodi yaad rehti hai?
