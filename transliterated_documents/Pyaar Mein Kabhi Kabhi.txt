Hum sab kisi na kisi ko chahte hai  aur is chahat mein hum yeh bhool jaate hai ki shayad hum mein bhi koi chahta hai
Taali do haath se bajti hai  ek haath se sirf tamacha padta hai
Hamesha achchi cheezein achche logon ke liye hote hai
Agar dil se hasna chahte ho  toh sankoch ki har seemao ko todh do
Zindagi mein khushi paane ke liye pyar ka hona zaroori hai
