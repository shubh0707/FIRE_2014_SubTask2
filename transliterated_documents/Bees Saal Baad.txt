Pyar karne waale jismon ke milan par nahi  aatmaon ke milan par bharosa karte hai
Badal ki taqdeer mein toh sirf garajna aur aasoon bahana likha hota hai
Pita ka hukum manna beti ka farz hai  lekin patni ko pati se alag rehne ka hukum agar bhagwan bhi de  toh usse bhi thukra dena patni ka dharam hai
Jis pyar mein tyaag ho, woh tyaag mahaan hota hai  aur usse bhi mahaan hota hai, tyaag karne waala
Kaante beejhne waale kaanto par hi chala karte hai  kaante beejhkar phoolon ki umeed moorkh kiya karte hai
Agar garajne se hi barbadi ka toofan uthne lage  toh barasne ki zaroorat nahi padti
Joh jeevan de sakta hai  woh jeevan le nahi sakta
Duniya ke dastoor paon ki zanjeer ban sakte hai, maut ki nahi  zamana zinda laashon ka milan rok sakta hai, aatmaon ke milan ko nahi rok sakta
Patthar par aasoon girne se, patthar pighalta nahi
Pyar andha hota hai  usse koi niyam, koi hadd dikhai nahi deti  woh kisi bhi sulukh ki parwah kiye baigar apni hadd se guzar jaata hai
Ghamand toh ek sheesha hai joh toot kar hi rehta hai
Insaanon ka paap ya toh aulad bankar samne aate hai  ya phir aulad ko bhugatne padte hai
